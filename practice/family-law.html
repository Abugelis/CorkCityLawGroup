Family Law

Family law matters can be complex and emotionally challenging. We provide clear, practical advice to help you make informed decisions and move forward with confidence.

What we can help with:

  • Separation agreements
  • Divorce proceedings
  • Child custody and access
  • Maintenance arrangements
  • Domestic violence applications

Separation and Divorce

Navigating a separation or divorce can be one of the most challenging periods in a person’s life. Our solicitors provide clear, practical guidance to help you understand your legal rights and options from the outset. We assist with separation agreements, judicial separation, and divorce proceedings, ensuring that your interests are protected at every stage.

We aim to resolve matters efficiently and with as little conflict as possible, whether through negotiation, mediation, or court proceedings where necessary. Our priority is to provide reassurance and clarity during what can often be an uncertain time.

Child Custody and Access

When children are involved, their welfare is always the primary concern. We advise clients on all aspects of child custody (guardianship), access arrangements, and parental rights. Our approach is focused on achieving fair and practical solutions that serve the best interests of the child while respecting the rights of both parents.

Whether you are seeking to formalise an agreement or resolve a dispute, we provide supportive and clear legal advice throughout the process. Where required, we can represent you in court to ensure that your position is effectively presented.

Maintenance and Financial Agreements

Financial arrangements are a key part of any separation or divorce. We advise on spousal maintenance, child maintenance, and the division of assets, helping you reach agreements that are fair and sustainable. Our solicitors work to ensure that all financial matters are addressed clearly and comprehensively.

Where agreements cannot be reached amicably, we can guide you through the legal process and represent your interests to achieve a just outcome. Our aim is to provide straightforward advice that allows you to plan for the future with confidence.

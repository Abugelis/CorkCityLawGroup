Employment Law

Employment issues can have a significant impact on your career or business. We provide straightforward legal advice to help you understand your rights and resolve workplace matters with confidence.

What we can help with:

  • Unfair dismissal claims
  • Employment contracts and policies
  • Workplace grievances and disputes
  • Redundancy and termination
  • Settlement agreements

Employment law can be complex and constantly evolving. We provide straightforward legal advice to both employees and employers, helping you understand your rights and obligations in the workplace.

Workplace Disputes

We advise on a wide range of workplace disputes, including unfair dismissal, disciplinary procedures, and grievance processes. Our aim is to resolve issues efficiently while protecting your legal position.

Where necessary, we can represent you in negotiations or before relevant tribunals and courts.

Contracts and Agreements

Clear and well-drafted contracts are essential in any employment relationship. We assist with reviewing and preparing employment contracts, ensuring that terms are fair, legally compliant, and clearly understood by all parties.

We also advise on restrictive covenants, confidentiality agreements, and other key employment terms.

Redundancy and Termination

We provide guidance on redundancy processes, termination of employment, and settlement agreements. Our focus is on ensuring that procedures are handled correctly and that your rights are fully protected.

Whether you are an employer managing organisational change or an employee facing redundancy, we offer clear and practical support throughout.
